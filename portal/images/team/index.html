Featured Transcriber

Lisa Bar Leib grew up in Cleveland, OH. She is married and has five children. Lisa has over 35 years of experience in the secretarial/legal secretarial profession and over 20 years experience as a transcriber. From 1995 to 1997, she attended Kentucky Career Institute where she took training courses to become a court reporter. Lisa has been part of the eScribers transcription team for its start in November 05. Her area of expertise is legal transcription. Lisa has achieved CET**D (Certified Electronic Transcriber - Digital) certification by the AAERT. Lisa has transcribed 70,000 pages for eScribers.
